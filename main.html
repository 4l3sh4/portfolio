MMU Eid Fiesta

➥ Assistant Head of Logistics • Apr 2025

I served as the Assistant Head of Logistics for MMU Eid Fiesta, a major cultural event organized by the Students' College Committee, in collaboration with six other clubs in Multimedia University. It was a challenging experience, but it allowed me to develop my organizational and leadership skills significantly.

To assist my head of division, I helped tracked those who have or haven't attended meetings, created documents to book items that are needed for this 2000-person event, and took up the responsibility to lead the team whenever the head was unavailable at the time.

One of the major challenges I experienced was when my head was unable to be contacted during the morning of the event itself, and the chairs and tables haven't been set up yet. Not wanting to waste time, I tried to lead the team with the best of my abilities on how the tables should be arranged, and help them set up the chairs. Thankfully, midway through, my head finally arrived during our set-up. Although our presumed time to finish setting up was a little delayed, we still managed to set up everything, thankfully, in time.

This experience made me realise how backup plans are important, and how important it is to step up for the sake of other people whenever things are dire. I also am grateful for having such an understanding team during this event, as they were not only hardworking, but also are willing to help even the other divisions that are low in manpower, such as the special task team at the time.
